## Executive Summary

Agentic AI in HRSD empowers HR teams to deliver exceptional employee experiences while reducing administrative burden. Rather than replacing HR professionals, AI handles routine inquiries, onboarding coordination, and compliance tracking, allowing HR teams to focus on employee development and strategic initiatives.

## 1. Employee Self-Service Agentic AI Use Cases

### Use Case 1.1: Intelligent HR Assistant Agent

**Business Value:**
- Reduce HR inquiries by 60%
- Reduce HR processing time by 70%
- Enable 24/7 employee support
- Improve employee satisfaction by 40%

**How It Works:**
1. **Employee Inquiry Analysis**
   - Understands employee questions
   - Identifies information needs
   - Provides relevant information
   - Guides through processes

2. **Self-Service Enablement**
   - Guides employees through self-service
   - Provides step-by-step instructions
   - Validates actions
   - Confirms completion

3. **HR Process Support**
   - Assists with HR processes
   - Collects required information
   - Routes to appropriate team
   - Tracks progress

4. **Policy Information**
   - Provides policy information
   - Explains benefits
   - Answers compliance questions
   - Provides guidance

5. **Escalation**
   - Escalates complex issues
   - Provides context
   - Enables smooth handoff
   - Tracks resolution

**Employee Productivity Impact:**
- **HR Teams:** 60% fewer routine inquiries
- **HR Staff:** Focus on strategic initiatives
- **Employees:** 24/7 support, instant answers
- **Time Saved:** 2-3 hours per HR person per day

**Example Scenario:**
```
Employee: "How do I request time off?"
AI Agent:
- Explains: Time off policy and process
- Guides: Step-by-step request submission
- Validates: Manager approval requirements
- Confirms: Request submitted successfully
Result: Employee completes in 5 minutes, HR not involved
```
