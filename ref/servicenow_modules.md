# microservices Suite of Applications - Modules & Descriptions

## Overview
microservices is a leading Application Platform as a Service (aPaaS) that provides a comprehensive suite of applications for digital workflow automation across IT operations, customer service, HR, field service, finance, and security.

---

## 1. IT Service Management (ITSM)

**Description:** Core suite for IT operations, service desks, and incident management. ITSM is the foundation of microservices, enabling organizations to deliver IT services aligned with business needs.

**Key Modules:**
- **Incident Management** – Logging, tracking, and resolving IT incidents to minimize downtime and restore services quickly
- **Problem Management** – Root cause analysis of recurring incidents to prevent future issues and improve system stability
- **Change Management** – Controlled management of IT changes with approval workflows, impact analysis, and rollback procedures
- **Request Management** – User service requests and fulfillment tracking with automated routing and SLA management
- **Service Catalog** – Self-service portal for users to request services and items with guided workflows
- **Knowledge Management** – Centralized documentation, FAQs, and troubleshooting guides for self-service resolution
- **Service Level Management (SLM)** – SLA tracking and monitoring to ensure service quality standards
- **Configuration Management Database (CMDB)** – Tracks IT infrastructure, relationships, and dependencies

**High-Level Use Cases:**
- IT help desk operations and ticket management
- Incident response and escalation workflows
- Change control and approval processes
- IT asset and infrastructure tracking
- Service delivery optimization

---

## 2. IT Operations Management (ITOM)

**Description:** Managing and monitoring the health of infrastructure and services in real-time. ITOM provides visibility into IT operations and enables proactive issue detection.

**Key Modules:**
- **Discovery** – Automatically identifies and maps servers, software, network devices, and cloud resources
- **Service Mapping** – Maps services and their dependencies across infrastructure for impact analysis
- **Event Management** – Correlates alerts into actionable events to reduce alert fatigue
- **Operational Intelligence** – AI/ML-powered anomaly detection and predictive insights for infrastructure health
- **Cloud Management** – Cloud provisioning, orchestration, and management across multiple cloud providers

**High-Level Use Cases:**
- Infrastructure monitoring and health checks
- Proactive issue detection and prevention
- Cloud operations and multi-cloud management
- Service dependency mapping and impact analysis
- Alert correlation and event normalization

---

## 3. IT Asset Management (ITAM)

**Description:** Manages hardware and software assets throughout their lifecycle. ITAM ensures compliance, optimizes costs, and provides visibility into asset utilization.

**Key Modules:**
- **Hardware Asset Management (HAM)** – Tracks physical devices, equipment, and hardware inventory
- **Software Asset Management (SAM)** – Manages software licenses, installations, and compliance
- **License Management** – Ensures compliance and optimizes licensing costs to avoid penalties
- **Contract Management** – Tracks vendor contracts, agreements, and renewal dates

**High-Level Use Cases:**
- Asset lifecycle management from procurement to retirement
- Software license compliance and optimization
- Cost reduction through asset utilization analysis
- Vendor contract and agreement management
- Hardware inventory tracking and depreciation

---

## 4. Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC)

**Description:** Enterprise-wide risk, policy, and compliance management. GRC helps organizations meet regulatory requirements and manage organizational risk.

**Key Modules:**
- **Policy and Compliance** – Define, enforce, and monitor organizational policies across the enterprise
- **Risk Management** – Identify, assess, and mitigate business risks with risk scoring and mitigation tracking
- **Audit Management** – Plan and execute internal and external audits with evidence collection
- **Vendor Risk Management** – Assess and monitor third-party vendor risks and compliance

**High-Level Use Cases:**
- Regulatory compliance (SOX, HIPAA, GDPR, ISO)
- Enterprise risk assessment and mitigation
- Internal and external audit management
- Policy enforcement and attestation
- Vendor risk and compliance monitoring

## 5. Customer Service Management (CSM)

**Description:** Helps external-facing support teams manage customer requests and interactions. CSM enables omnichannel customer support and self-service capabilities.

**Key Modules:**
- **Case Management** – Track and resolve customer issues with full case history and context
- **Customer Portal** – Self-service portal for customers to submit, track, and resolve cases
- **Virtual Agent** – AI-powered chatbot for automated customer support and issue resolution
- **Entitlements & SLAs** – Define service levels, customer entitlements, and SLA tracking
- **Communities** – Community forums for peer-to-peer support and knowledge sharing

**High-Level Use Cases:**
- Customer support operations and case resolution
- Omnichannel customer interactions (chat, email, phone)
- Customer self-service and knowledge base
- SLA management and performance tracking
- Community-driven support and peer assistance

## 7. Field Service Management (FSM)

**Description:** Coordinates field service operations including scheduling, dispatching, and mobile work execution. FSM enables organizations to optimize field technician productivity and customer satisfaction.

**Key Modules:**
- **Work Order Management** – Create, assign, and manage field service work orders
- **Service Scheduling** – Assign work to technicians with AI-powered optimization and routing
- **Mobile Work Execution** – AI-powered mobile app for field technicians with offline capabilities
- **Workforce Optimization** – Optimize technician assignments, routing, and capacity planning
- **Predictive Maintenance** – Proactive servicing based on asset conditions and maintenance history
- **Territory Planning** – Organize service territories and technician assignments
- **Field Service Marketplace** – Connect with contractors and external service providers

**High-Level Use Cases:**
- Field technician dispatch and scheduling
- Proactive maintenance and asset monitoring
- First-time fix optimization
- Mobile workforce management
- Contractor and external resource management
- Service territory optimization
